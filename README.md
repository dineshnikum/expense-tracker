# Expense Tracker

A modern, comprehensive financial management dashboard designed to help you track expenses, manage budgets, and visualize your financial health with style.

## ğŸš€ Features

- **Smart Dashboard**: Get a real-time overview of your Total Balance, Income, and Expenses with trend indicators.
- **Financial Health Tracking**: Monitor your monthly spending against a set budget ($2,500 default) with visual health scores.
- **Savings Goals**: Track your progress towards specific goals (e.g., Gaming Setup) with dynamic progress bars.
- **Transaction Management**:
    - Categorize transactions (Food, Transport, Housing, Entertainment, Shopping, Income).
    - Add, edit, and delete transactions easily.
    - Auto-calculated timestamps.
- **Interactive Charts**: Visualize spending habits with Category Pie Charts and Weekly Activity Charts.
- **Dark Mode**: Comes with a sleek, meaningful Dark Mode enabled by default for comfortable viewing.
- **Demo Data**: Includes a "Bachelor Boy" profile with realistic transaction history to demonstrate app capabilities instantly.
- **Data Persistence**: All your data is saved locally, so you pick up right where you left off.
